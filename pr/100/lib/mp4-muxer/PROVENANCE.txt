mp4-muxer — in-browser MP4 multiplexer for WebCodecs-encoded video.

Used by: ui/export-modals.js (showExport3DVideoModal) to mux H.264 frames
encoded via the WebCodecs VideoEncoder into a downloadable .mp4. Loaded as a
plain <script> tag in index.html; exposes the global `Mp4Muxer` (with
`Muxer` and `ArrayBufferTarget`).

Version: 5.2.1 (UMD build).
Re-fetch:
  curl -sL -o lib/mp4-muxer/mp4-muxer.js \
    https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/mp4-muxer@5.2.1/build/mp4-muxer.js

Committed (not gitignored) — same policy as lib/h5wasm and lib/sleap-io.
